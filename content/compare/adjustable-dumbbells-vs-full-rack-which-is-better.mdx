## Bottom line

If your primary constraint is space, adjustable dumbbells are usually the better first purchase. A full rack makes sense when you have dedicated room and prioritize top-end loading convenience.

## Decision framework

- Choose adjustable dumbbells if you train in a shared room.
- Choose a rack if your training volume and loading needs are consistently high and space is not a concern.
