## Verdict
Inspire FTX is the strongest anchor choice for compact cable-based training. It balances footprint, cable quality, and movement range better than most all-in-one alternatives in this size class.

### Buy this if
- You want a cable-first machine with broad movement coverage.
- You care about smooth transitions between exercises.

### Skip this if
- Your budget is limited and value is the top priority.
- You prefer plate-loaded setups over cable systems.

## Key specs
- Dual-stack functional trainer format
- Adjustable pulley positions for varied movement patterns
- Compact footprint for home gym rooms

## Space and setup notes
- Verify top clearance and side space for full cable range.
- Assembly is more manageable with two people.
- Plan machine position around bench and walk-lane access.

## Pros
- High-quality cable feel
- Strong functional exercise coverage
- Efficient use of floor space

## Cons
- Expensive versus value hybrids
- Setup and transport are heavier tasks
