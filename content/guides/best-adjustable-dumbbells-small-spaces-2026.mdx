## How to choose adjustable dumbbells for small spaces

Prioritize **footprint**, **adjustment speed**, and **reliability**. In apartments, equipment that stores cleanly and changes quickly between exercises usually drives better adherence.

## Weight range guidance

- Beginners: 5-30 lb is often enough for months of progress.
- Intermediate lifters: look for 5-50 lb minimum.
- Advanced home users: consider expandable systems up to 70-90 lb.

## What matters most in real use

A dumbbell that is slightly heavier and a little slower to change can still beat a lighter option if it feels stable and survives frequent workouts.
