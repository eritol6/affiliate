## What apartment setups need

Small-space gyms succeed when they are easy to put away and quick to start. The less friction you have before training, the more consistent your routine becomes.

## Noise and floor protection

Use rubber tiles or thick mats under benches and dumbbell trays to reduce both vibration and neighbor complaints.
