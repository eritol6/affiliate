## Verdict
The Powerline PFT100 is the most straightforward way to get commercial-style cable training in a compact footprint. Dual 160 lb stacks, smooth pulley travel, and stable construction make it suitable for serious strength work without taking over a full garage.

### Buy this if
- You want cable-first training with dual independent stacks.
- You prioritize stable frame feel over all-in-one gimmicks.

### Skip this if
- You need built-in rack features in the same machine.
- Your room cannot support dedicated floor space for a fixed trainer.

## Key specs
- Dual independent 160 lb weight stacks
- Compact dual-stack functional trainer layout
- Cable-first movement coverage for push, pull, and accessory work

## Space and setup notes
- Measure width, depth, and cable clearance before ordering.
- Leave side clearance for full pulley travel and safe transitions.
- Assembly is manageable but time-intensive, with frame alignment steps.

## Pros
- Dual independent weight stacks

- Recognizable brand quality
- Stable frame

## Cons
- No built-in rack features
